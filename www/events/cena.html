Cena a las Seis

Cena a las Seis, or "Dinner at Six," is an annual LCC-orgnaized Family Weekend event where we bring MIT students and their families together for a semi-formal evening of Latino cuisine and student performances and presentations. The student groups that annually participate in the organization and excecution ofthis event are the Latino Cultural Center, La Unión Chicana por Aztlán, Mujeres Latinas, the MIT Chapter of the Society of Mexican American Engineers and Scientists, the Association of Puerto Rican Students, Teatro Latino, and the MIT Chapter of the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers.

This year, Cena a las Seis 2010 will be held on Saturday, October 16th at 6PM in Morss Hall of Walker Memorial.

Please check back at the beginning of the fall semester for more details on speakers and performers for the event. We hope to see you there!
